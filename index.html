Kenya

Kenya is located in the east of Africa, it is known for the diverse wildlife,national parks, and rich cultural heritage. It borders the Indian ocean, lake Victoria, Ethiopia,Somalia, South Sudan, Tanzania and Uganda. The capital is Nairobi, a very beautiful city. The first temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints has been erected in Kenya in 2024.Since independence Kenya has had 5 presidents, who have all in their unique way made Kenya better. Kenya is divided in counties, my favourite one is Laikipia county, its one underated county with very beautiful sites to visit. I love a place called Nanyuki, it is not that big but its a place you can get all you need, I lived there for almost two years and it was the best experience ever.
